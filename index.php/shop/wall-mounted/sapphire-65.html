Description

Diamond is “heavy artillery” of water purification which is able to cope even with high polluted water. Two electrochemical reactors provide perfect activation properties but also free it from any kinds and forms of toxic organic compounds, heavy metal ions, iron and colloidal particles. Water molecules take on a balanced structure, the water is free rom the harmful influence of the water pipes, and overall the water gains a fresh taste.
