ECAS STO 3 in 1

STO the most popular model of Emerald. The success of the installation is due to its functionality: the service station is able not only to efficiently clean plumbing, but also to produce the famous living and dead water, which is actively used to prevent the treatment of a wide range of diseases.
Description

RedOx


Redox potential (RedOx) of water using the HUNDRED can be adjusted. In the RedOx purification mode, the water after treatment with SRT is -150 mV, which favorably affects its absorption by the cells of the body. But if you turn the switch, the additional function of the water purifier is activated - live and dead water. RedOx of living water reaches -250-300 mV, and dead water - up to +500 mV.

 

Water purification

 

The water purification in ECAS STO is identical in principle to the Sapphire water purifier technology. In the plastic case of marble color there is one electrochemical reactor - the heart of the water purifier. After treatment in an electrochemical reactor, activated water enters the mixing chamber, where all chemical processes are completed. In addition, the installation has two quartz chambers, which bring the degree of purification to perfection. 
With the help of the original design and set parameters, the electrochemical reactor in the STO installation is also capable of receiving live and dead water.

The unique device of the ECAS installations makes it possible not to accumulate harmful substances and bacteria inside the water purifier, but to drain them.

STO is always connected to your tap. With the help of a special nozzle, you can turn on the mode you need - “tap water” or “clear water”. In order to get drinking water, you only need to send tap water through the installation by turning the switch. If you turn the switch on the side panel of ECAS, then an additional function of the water purifier is activated, with which you can get live and dead water.
