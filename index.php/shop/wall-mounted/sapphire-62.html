Description

KFTO is the most functional model of Emerald. CFTO is not only able to effectively clean the tap, but also produce the famous living and dead water, which is actively used to prevent the treatment of a wide range of diseases. In contrast to the HUNDRED model, not one but two electrochemical reactors are installed in the CFTO housing, which ensures the production of water with almost any RedOx and perfect cleaning quality. This is a water purifier for those who not only like to drink tasty clean water, but also want to improve their health with it.

RedOx

Redox potential (RedOx) of water using the HUNDRED can be adjusted. In the RedOx purification mode, the water after treatment with SRT is equal to -200 mV, which favorably affects its absorption by the cells of the body. But if you turn the switch, the additional function of the water purifier is activated - live and dead water. RedOx of living water reaches -300-400 mV (provides unsurpassed antioxidant protection for the body), and dead water - up to +500 mV.

Water purification

The water purification in Emerald-CFTO is identical in principle to the technology of the Diamond water purifier. In the plastic case of marble color there are two electrochemical reactors - the heart of the water purifier. 
After anodic treatment in reactors, water enters the microbubble chamber, where it undergoes micro electrophysical gases dissolved in water - oxygen, ozone, hydrogen. They are saturated with water during the preceding stages of electrochemical treatment. The use of a microbubble reactor instead of a mixing reactor significantly enhances the removal efficiency of organic compounds, microorganisms, microbial toxins. 
With the help of the original design and the specified parameters, the electrochemical reactor in the CFTO installation is also capable of receiving live and dead water.

The unique device of the Emerald installations makes it possible not to accumulate harmful substances and bacteria inside the water purifier, but to drain them.

KFTO is always connected to your tap. With the help of a special nozzle, you can turn on the mode you need - “tap water” or “clear water”. In order to get drinking water, you only need to send tap water through the installation by turning the switch. If you turn the switch on the side panel of Emerald, then an additional function of the water purifier is activated, with which you can get live and dead water.
