Industrial

 

 

Emerald-PROM (for enterprises, private houses, cafes) is a plant for cleaning and activating water, designed for large capacities. Emerald-PROM (for enterprises, private houses, cafes) will be indispensable where high-quality water purification on an industrial scale is required. The water purifier can be installed in the enterprise, as well as built into the water supply of a building, private house, or enterprise. Thanks to the “Diamond” type purification technology, Emerald-PROM (for enterprises, private houses, cafes) guarantees high-quality purification and activation of water with a decrease in the redox potential.

Water purified in Izumrude-PROM (for enterprises, private houses, cafes) from all types and forms of toxic organic compounds, heavy metal ions, iron, and colloidal suspensions. The structure of the molecules is leveled, water gets rid of the harmful effects of water pipes and acquires a refreshing taste. Unlike most industrial filters, Emerald-PROM (for enterprises, private houses, cafes) does not just drain the water, leaving the bacteria on the sorbents, but completely decomposes and removes harmful substances into the drainage. Therefore, Emerald-PROM (for enterprises, private houses, cafes) is a high-quality and comprehensive solution to the problems of industrial water treatment.

RedOx

The redox potential (RedOx) of water with the help of Emerald-PROM is reduced from + 200-400 to -200 millivolts, which is equal to the value of human RedOx. This allows the body to spend almost no energy on the absorption of such water. Read more about RedOx in the technology section -> emerald water

Easy to use

The Emerald-PROM installation (for enterprises, private houses, cafes) works in semi-automatic mode. To activate the installation, you must click the "Enable" button and open the tap. The time of the installation to the operating mode is only 3 minutes, which allows you to quickly stop and start the installation. Connection installation does not require additional devices and details. Change the cartridges and filter elements in the installation is not required. For optimum performance, the water purifier should be rinsed with an acidic solution after every 50,000 liters of purified water.

Installation Care

After every 50,000 liters of water, the water purifier must be rinsed with an acidic solution so that the reactors of the plant are clean of calcium deposits. This is done only for the best flow of water - calcareous deposits clog the reactor and reduce water pressure. Calcium deposits are absolutely safe: they are the notorious scale on the bottoms of kettles.
